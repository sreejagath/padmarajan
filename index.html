P. Padmarajan (23 May 1945 – 24 January 1991) was an Indian film maker, screenwriter and author who was known for his landmark work in Malayalam literature and Malayalam cinema.

Movies

Thoovanathumbikal

Thoovanathumbikal is a 1987 Indian Malayalam-language romantic drama film written and directed by P. Padmarajan, based partly on his own novel Udakappola.

Namukku Parkkan Munthirithoppukal

Namukku Parkkan Munthirithoppukal is a 1986 Indian Malayalam-language romance drama film written and directed by Padmarajan based on the 1986 novel Nammukku Gramangalil Chennu Rapparkkam by K. K. Sudhakaran.

Innale

Innale is a 1990 Malayalam Psychological Romantic drama film, written and directed by P. Padmarajan with Suresh Gopi, Shobhana, Jayaram, and Srividya in pivotal roles.
